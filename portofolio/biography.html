About Me

After I graduate,I want to work mostly as a mobile app developer and data analyst. In addition to programming, I am a well-rounded individual who enjoys reading books, designing bags and loves to explore new places and cultures. I have desire to make a positive impact on the world, and I believe that software engineering is a powerful tool that can be used to solve complex problems and improve people's lives.
